Purpose

At CancerCare for Kids, our mission is to eradicate pediatric cancer through unwavering dedication to funding cutting-edge research and our state-of-the-art facilities and programs. Committed to providing hope and support, we strive to empower the scientific community in their quest for breakthroughs and ensure that every child facing cancer receives the innovative treatments and compassionate care they deserve.

Knowledge

"Pediatric cancer awareness is essential in promoting early detection and timely intervention." - St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital. This is why CancerCare for Kids provides many educational programs in order to achieve better outcomes for our young patients.

Support

"Community support is a powerful force." - American Cancer Society. Through CancerCare for Kids’s mentoring program, experienced cancer survivors and their families connect with current cancer patients and their families to offer guidance and be a source of inspiration.

Care

"Caring for pediatric cancer patients is about preserving innocence and nurturing dreams." - World Health Organization. This is why CancerCare for Kids makes sure to thoughtfully curate care packages in order to support cancer patients and their families, providing comfort during tough times.
